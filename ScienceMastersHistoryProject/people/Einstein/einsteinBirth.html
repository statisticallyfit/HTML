Albert Einstein was born at Ulm, in Wurttemberg, Germany, on March 14, 1879. His sister Maria was born two years later, the two experienced a very close friendship. Their parents were Hermann and Pauline, a wealthy Jewish couple. Even as a young child, his passion for music and science rivaled those of children his age. He would later grow up to become not only one of the world's famous scientists, but also a symbol of humanity and perseverance.

When he was 76 years old in the year 1955, Einstein died from an aortic aneurism, in Princeton, New Jersey.
