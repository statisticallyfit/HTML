ELEMENTARY

When I was in elementary school, I enjoyed playing with my three best friends. Together, we learned how to spell, practiced reading comprehension, and also studied fractions and long-division.
Later in grades four to five, I came to a new school and there enjoyed learning about medieval times and wattle-and-daub houses, and also strove to master mathematics. In this school, I also met a new girl from Russia, and we soon became best friends, inviting each other for sleepovers and building forts out of pillows and chairs at our houses. Those years, I also met two of my favorite teachers in that school.
