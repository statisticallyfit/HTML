~ About Moi ~

My name is Ana-Maria and I am a student at K12 International Academy online, based in America.
In athletics, I enjoy tennis, badminton, swimming, skating, skiing, horseback riding, and rock-climbing. Lucky for me, I am coordinated enough so that I have a knack for learning and playing new sports.
Artistically speaking, I thrive when painting, sketching, and writing creative fiction. One of my best friends who has also attended K12 has helped me build my writing skills, bit by bit, and we often helpfully critique each others' fiction writing. Additionally, I also enjoy playing the flute and listening to Celtic and violin music.
