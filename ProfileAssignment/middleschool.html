MIDDLE SCHOOL

Middle school is when I met my favorite teacher, Mr. Neville. Always in a dangerous mood, he still never forgot to crack jokes every now and then. In fact, with Mr. Neville as a teacher, these jokes came all too often; he would make the whole class laugh during spelling tests as he described the clumsy endeavors of his, and our, favorite fiction character. Grade six was also the year my class and I went to Quebec on a school field trip. I was thoroughly fascinated by this province, since its cobbled stones, small bakeries and shops, cathedrals, museums, and souvenir shops reminded me of Europe.
