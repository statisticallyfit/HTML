PRESCHOOL

My preschool days included running around outside with fellow explorers and going on neighborhood field trips with the teachers and other kinder-gardeners. Also, once my babysitter picked me up, we would go the park near her house. Or, if I was lucky, she would bring me to the pool, where I would receive swimming lessons. Some days, instead of the swimming pool, my babysitter would take me to an ankle-deep basin in which I would cool my feet by splashing about when the sun was hot. When my parents picked me up after the arrived from work, I would tell them all about my adventures.
