INFANT

As an infant, my favorite activities included sleeping, crying, and slurping warm milk. And of course, playing with toys. My grandparents often would come from Romania for six months and babysit me during the day while my parents went to work. They taught me how to paint, and also taught me Romanian from old folktale stories. When it snowed, my grandparents took me to the park, and, looking back now, I remember those days as a blur of snow-white and much laughter.
